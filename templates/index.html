  • Assists in medication adjustments
  • Enhances overall quality of life

What is the purpose of this diabetes monitoring tool?

Our tool helps you monitor your blood sugar and other health indicators using advanced AI, making it easier to manage diabetes and make informed decisions about your health.

Do I need to be a doctor or understand complex medical terms to use this?

No, you don't need any medical background or technical knowledge. Our system is designed to be simple and user-friendly, providing clear explanations and recommendations anyone can understand.

How does the AI analyze my health data?

The AI uses machine learning models trained on medical data to interpret your input and provide personalized insights and suggestions for managing your diabetes.

Is my personal health information stored or shared?

No, your data is processed securely and only kept temporarily while you use the service. We do not store or share your personal health information.

Can I use this tool on my phone or tablet?

Yes, our service is accessible from any device with a web browser, including smartphones and tablets, so you can check your health status anytime, anywhere.

Is this tool a replacement for professional medical advice?

No, this tool is designed to support your health monitoring, but it does not replace advice from your doctor or healthcare provider. Always consult a professional for medical decisions.
